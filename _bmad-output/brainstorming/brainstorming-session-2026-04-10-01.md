## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** Career breakout strategy with focus on achieving clarity on direction

**Recommended Techniques:**

- **Five Whys (deep):** Drill into root motivations and root blockers — why stuck, why AI, why 2-3x. Prevents building solutions for the wrong goal.
- **First Principles Thinking (creative):** Strip away all assumptions from idea brief and career advice. Rebuild from fundamental truths about what actually gets someone hired in AI.

**AI Rationale:** Session arc designed as DIG → STRIP → REVERSE-ENGINEER. Moves from emotional/motivational clarity through analytical deconstruction into concrete actionable direction. Specifically chosen because user needs to cut through overwhelm (9-module spec) to find the real path.

**The Critical Insight — Two Parallel Loops, Not Sequential Phases:**

```
WRONG: Learn → Build → Perfect → THEN Interview
RIGHT: Build + Interview SIMULTANEOUSLY from Day 1
       Projects feed interview answers
       Failed interviews feed project priorities
       BOTH loops accelerate each other
```
